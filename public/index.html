Take a leap of faith...or hang around by guiding the Pope through the church by swinging from chandeliers in this dazzling, challenging and fun game!

Try to get the highest score from everyone else by swinging from chandeliers, collecting coins and avoiding the bats!

Features an infinite number of chandeliers to swing from with varying heights for more daring swings and tricky challenges avoiding high-rise church beams and flying bats set to knock you off-track.

  • Features an unlimited level and chandeliers to swing from.
  • Collect coins and score points.
  • 2 power ups including slow-time for precision swings and holy shield to gain time limited invincibility.
  • Real rope physics for challenging game play
